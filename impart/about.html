What We Do

Impact Project is a response to the growing need for financial accountability in the oil sector.The company was formed with the aim of engaging the general public in the allocation and disbursement of revenue to major sectors of the economy. With the Impact Project solution, an average Nigerian can observe, analyze, interpret, execute and share his recommendations on summary reports of financial flows.

Thus it provides an avenue for the government to see the need of the citizens and allocate revenue to the major sectors affecting the masses. It also gives the government an insight into the effects of the variances in the revenue to the economy.
